## **Crab Music**

A lightweight and efficient music downloader for popular platforms.

### Overview

Crab Music is a simple yet powerful tool designed to download high-quality music from leading streaming services, including YouTube, Deezer, and Spotify. Our mission is to provide a seamless music downloading experience while continuously expanding our support to more platforms and enhancing the quality of downloaded content.

### Features

* **High-quality downloads**: Get the best audio quality available for your favorite songs.
* **Simple usage**: Just write the song name you want to download, and Crab Music will take care of the rest.
* **Multiple sources**: Download playlists, albums, and individual songs from YouTube, Deezer, and Spotify.
* **Fast and efficient**: Built with Rust, Crab Music ensures a lightning-fast experience without compromising on performance.
* **Cross-platform**: Crab Music is available on Windows, macOS, and Linux, ensuring a consistent experience across all platforms.
* **No ads**: Enjoy an ad-free experience while downloading your favorite music.
